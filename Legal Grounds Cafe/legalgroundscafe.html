About Us

Legal Grounds Café is a welcoming neighborhood spot offering specialty coffee, hearty breakfast and lunch options, and flavorful Middle Eastern favorites. Conveniently located near the Middlesex County Courthouse and within walking distance of the New Brunswick Train Station, our cafe is a go-to destination for locals, commuters, and visitors alike.

Recently renovated, we take pride in serving everything from handcrafted espresso drinks and breakfast sandwiches to gyros, falafel, and fresh wraps—perfect for dining in or taking on the go. With affordable prices and friendly service, Legal Grounds Cafe is proud to be part of the New Brunswick community.
